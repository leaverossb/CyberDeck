CyberDeck

Tactical Network Defense

Can you learn the basics of cybersecurity while being distracted by an easy to play card game? Some people would say “no”. But we don’t care about those opinions. Download CyberDeck for free and see for yourself.

This may be the only game where cybersecurity experts can play against people without the slightest interest in cybersecurity, computers, or reading. If you’re the person thinking, “that’s nonsense, how could a non-techy person possibly pose a challenge to a cybersecurity expert in a game solely based around cybersecurity?”, don't take my word for it. Watch the how to play videos, download the files and see for yourself!
